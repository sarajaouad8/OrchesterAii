Features

Everything you need to automate project management

From document analysis to team coordination, OrchestraAI handles the operational heavy lifting so you can focus on building.

PDF Intelligence

Upload any project brief, SOW, or requirements doc. Our AI parses every page and extracts actionable tasks automatically.

Instant Task Generation

Receive a structured task list with priorities, deadlines, and dependencies generated in seconds — not hours of manual work.

Smart Team Assignment

AI matches tasks to team members based on skill sets, workload, and availability for balanced resource allocation.

Real-time Dashboards

Track progress with live metrics. Monitor velocity, blockers, and milestones across every project in one view.

Continuous Sync

Integrates with Jira, Linear, Asana, and Notion. Changes sync bi-directionally so your tools stay in harmony.
